How to Join?

Here is the steps you need to go through to join stc Digital Community Platform

01

Use your stc Email

Enter your stc email address to populate your profile on the community

02

Fill the questionnaire

Go through a set of questions to help the selection committe* understand your intentions and skills.

*You will receive a confirmation email for acceptance

03

Add and serve your friends

After joining you will be able to add your friends and relative to your profile and act as stc ambassador for them.

04

Enjoy your rewards

Helping customers, solving quizzes and sharing helpfull answers for other community members will be rewarded with points and badges.

*Understand the rewarding here.

How can the Ambassador works?

Help stc customers understand more about services and products by sharing usefull information with them

Submit and follow-up with customers complaints and inquires

Ask and answer questions with other ambassadors in the community

Easily Monitor Everything

Track your customers engagement and satisfaction and your points and rewards.
